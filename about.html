About Me

Hello! I'm Rochelle T. Cabusao, 21 years old, a dedicated student of Bachelor of Library and Information Science (BLIS) in the FSUU. With a strong interest in information management and library services, I'm committed to continuous learning and understanding how information can be organized and accessed effectively.

My Journey

I'm a student assistant in Jansen Library. Through my studies and working in the library, I've gained insights into cataloging, reference services, information literacy, and the evolving role of libraries in the digital age.

Academic Interests

• Information Literacy & Community Education
• Research & Critical Thinking

Beyond Studies

When I'm not studying, I enjoy reading, expanding my knowledge about how information support various communities. I believe in the importance of libraries as spaces for learning and the power of information accessibility to create positive change.
